Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

How long until I see results?
Most customers notice improvements in skin texture and radiance within 2-4 weeks of consistent use. More significant results like reduced fine lines and improved firmness typically appear around 6-8 weeks. Everyone's skin is different, which is why we offer a 60-day guarantee.
Is it safe for my eyes?
Yes! Our mask includes medical-grade eye shields that block the light completely. The wavelengths we use (633nm and 830nm) are also extensively studied and considered safe. Simply keep your eyes closed during treatment and use the included shields for extra protection.
Can I use it with my skincare products?
For best results, use on clean, bare skin—light penetrates better without products blocking it. After your session, apply your serums and moisturizers as normal. Avoid using strong actives like retinol immediately before treatment if your skin is sensitive.
How does the 60-day guarantee work?
We want you to love your results. Use the mask as directed for up to 60 days. If you're not happy with your results, contact us for a full refund—no restocking fees, no hassle. We'll even cover return shipping within Australia.
How often should I use it?
For best results, use 3-5 times per week. Each session is just 10 minutes, and the mask auto-shuts off when complete. Consistency matters more than frequency—find a routine that works for your schedule and stick with it.
